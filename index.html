I am a PhD student at the LUCS Robotics Group and affiliated with Social Robotics group at The Division of Robotics, Perception and Learning at KTH. My main research interest is the role non-verbal signals play in establishing trust relationships between humans and social robots and how these non-verbal signals affect the way we perceive and interact with social robots.
